Who checked out the book 'The Hobbit’? 

Answer: Anand Beck

Query: SELECT member.name FROM book, checkout_item, member WHERE member.id = checkout_item.member_id AND book.id = checkout_item.book_id AND book.title = 'The Hobbit';

How many people have not checked out anything?

Answer: 37

Query: SELECT COUNT(member.name) FROM member WHERE member.id NOT IN (SELECT member_id FROM checkout_item);

Who has checked out more than 1 item?  Tip: Research the GROUP BY syntax.

Answer: 
Anand Beck
Frank Smith

Query:  SELECT member.name FROM checkout_item, member WHERE member.id = checkout_item.member_id GROUP BY member.name HAVING COUNT(member.name) > 1;
